Who Am I?




Hi, my name is Alex! I'm a graphic designer with a passion for package, logo and brand design, I also enjoy web design. I am currently in college studying graphic design and hope to continue my studies with brand design as well. I specialize in the Adobe Creative Suite and love experimenting with colour, typography and different layout styles to create unique designs. When I'm not designing, you'll find me capturing inspiration through photography or reading some of my favourite books. I'm always excited to collaborate on creative projects!
