Who Am I?




Hi, my name is Alex! I'm a graphic designer with a passion for logo, package, brand design, and a growing interest in web design. I'm currently studying graphic design in college and plan to continue focusing on brand design. I specialize in the Adobe Creative Suite and enjoy experimenting with colour, typography, and layout to create thoughtful, unique visuals. When I'm not designing, I'm often searching for inspiration through photography or reading some of my favourite books and sipping on hot cocoa. I'm always excited to collaborate on creative projects!
